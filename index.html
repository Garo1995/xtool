FAQ

What's the difference between F2 Ultra, F2 Ultra (Single), and F1 Ultra?

F2 Ultra:

Equipped with both a 60W MOPA fiber laser and a 40W diode laser. Best for users who want maximum versatility in materials and processes. Supports color marking on metals, deep engraving, cutting, and processing on non-metal materials.

F2 Ultra(single):

Equipped with only the 60W MOPA fiber laser. Better for users who mainly work with metal materials.

F1 Ultra uses a Q-switched fiber laser, equipped with both a 20W fiber laser and a 20W diode laser, a single camera, speeds up to 10.000 mm/s. Best for standard metal marking and entry-level needs.

What’s the max engraving depth or cutting thickness for metals?

The F2 Ultra can cut brass up to 2mm, stainless steel up to 2mm, aluminum up to 1mm. Engraving depth depends on the material and settings, but the 60W MOPA fiber laser ensures deep and precise results across a wide range of metals.

What materials can be engraved with F2 Ultra?

F2 Ultra can engrave almost all materials. Metals like gold, silver, aluminum, stainless steel (with multiple colors), platinum, titanium (with multiple colors), and others. Non-metals like wood, acrylic, plastic, slate, glass, paper, cardboard, and others.

What is the warranty of this machine?

F2 Ultra comes with a 12 months warranty.

How large is the working area provided by xTool F2 Ultra?

The working area is 220 × 220 mm and 220×500 mm with the conveyor.
